After Cataract Surgery

Recovery after cataract surgery is usually smooth and comfortable. Following the care instructions will help your eye heal faster and prevent complications.

Things You Should Do

  • Use eye drops as prescribed
  • Wear the protective eye shield while sleeping
  • Keep the operated eye clean and dry
  • Attend all follow-up check-ups
  • Wear sunglasses in bright sunlight

Things You Should Avoid

  • Do not rub or press the operated eye
  • Avoid water entering the eye for 1 week
  • Avoid lifting heavy weights
  • Avoid bending your head too much
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol
  • Do not drive until your doctor permits

Warning Signs — Visit the Hospital If You Notice

  • Severe pain in the eye
  • Sudden decrease in vision
  • Excessive redness or swelling
  • Continuous watering
  • Flashes of light or floaters

Eye Drop Instructions

Use only the prescribed eye drops and follow the instructed schedule carefully.
